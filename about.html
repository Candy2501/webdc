About Dhooli™

Dhooli™ began as a vision nearly thirty years ago, rooted in a commitment
to provide effective and affordable cleaning solutions for both household
and professional needs. In 2024, this longstanding dream became a reality
with the launch of Dhooli™—a brand dedicated to manufacturing,
distributing, and marketing high-quality, essential cleaning products.

Our extensive product range covers a broad spectrum of needs, with
solutions for floors, surfaces, laundry, dishes, toilets, and more—each
designed to ensure cleanliness, ease of use, and sustainability. At Dhooli™,
we are committed to making spaces cleaner, fresher, and healthier by
delivering products that are reliable, high-performing, and offer exceptional
value. From our modest beginnings to today, our mission remains clear: to
provide Indian households and businesses with trusted, effective cleaning solutions.

We’ve expanded our reach and established a strong presence in both retail
and B2B sectors. Dhooli™ products are available at major retail outlets
across Durgapur, Asansol, and Raniganj, and our B2B network supports a
diverse array of institutions and industries, including schools, hospitals,
restaurants, hotels, colleges, and factories.
